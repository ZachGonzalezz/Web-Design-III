Polaris Mission

Polaris was orginially designed with the idea of solving the problem of lack of mentorship for youth. If you are young without a network it is hard to find a mentorship to get advice.

Expand Mentor Avalibility

Trade

  • Solve the issue of students not easily be able to find a mentor and get started in their career
  • Solve the problem of students going to college without actually knowing what they are getting into
  • Help kids who want to get started early have a platform to get advice and go

Encourage Student Tech Projects

Trade

  • Have place to post your project ideas and get students your age to join in and help
  • Have ecosystem to find these startup ideas by kids and be able to join them
  • Connect student entrepunears to mentors and assistance to help them build something

Platform to network

Trade

  • Have a platform in which students and mentors are able to interact
  • Be able to find other students who are interested into technology
  • Be able to find connection and grow your network in technology as a student
